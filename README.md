# Sovereign Execution Authority (SEA)

Execution Governance for Real-Time AI Systems

A governance substrate for AI systems operating in real-world environments.

## Overview

Sovereign Execution Authority (SEA) ensures that AI systems remain authorized at the exact moment they execute real-world actions.

As AI moves from decision to execution, a critical gap emerges.

SEA closes that gap.

## The Problem: Authority Drift

Between decision and execution:
- Context shifts  
- Authority expires  
- Data degrades  

Yet systems continue to act.

## The Solution: Execution Governance

SEA introduces a control point at execution.

Every action is evaluated in real time:

- Authority  
- Context  
- Admissibility  

### Outcomes
- Approved  
- Denied  
- Halted  

## System Position

SEA operates at the execution boundary.

- Non-bypassable  
- Real-time  
- Auditable  

## The Third Mind Framework

- Human Intelligence sets intent  
- Artificial Intelligence makes decisions  
- Execution Intelligence validates in real time  

SEA is the execution layer.
